The 6 Systematic Exit & Roll Reasons

Trend Exit
EMA-50 Crossover: Triggered at 3:45 PM close when the stock price falls below the 50-day EMA by 5% or more. Liquidates underlying stock and option.
VRP Halt
Underpriced Volatility: VRP z-score drops below -1.0. Halts all option writing and liquidates stock/options immediately to lock in capital to 100% cash.
Early Roll
Extrinsic Exhaustion: Decay of the option's extrinsic value to ≤ 15% of the entry premium. Triggers buyback of active option and writes a new contract.
Stop Loss
Static Cushion Exit: Intraday low touches $S_{ ext{stop}} = 0.92 imes (S_{ ext{entry}} - P_{ ext{entry}})$. Stock exits pessimistically, option closed at EOD Ask.
Expiration
Option Settlement: Contract matures. Shares are called away if Stock > Strike, else the option expires worthless and stock shares are retained.
Assignment
Early Assignment Hazard: Probabilistic assignment risk triggered daily when DTE ≤ 10 and option extrinsic ≤ $0.25.
